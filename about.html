Our Story & Philosophy

Committed to fresh farm supply chain, biosecure nesting, and food safety standards.

From Humble Roots to Leading Supplier

Founded with the core goal of supplying unprocessed, fresh, hygienic eggs, Samarth Eggs Center has grown from a single poultry distribution channel into a reliable commercial supply partner servicing over 500 hotels, retail bakeries, and food companies.

We source daily from our modern, Bio-Secured farms where layers are cared for in stress-free conditions, fed a customized nutrient-balanced mash, and undergo clean mechanical packaging procedures.

Our Mission

To deliver farm-to-table freshness daily, optimizing delivery logistics for food manufacturers while maintaining the highest international hygiene and nutritional standards.

Our Vision

To establish Samarth Eggs Center as the gold standard of premium egg supply nationwide, leading through sustainable farming, automated sorting, and cold storage innovations.

Modern Warehouse & Cold Storage

Egg freshness is highly sensitive to external temperatures. To preserve the high protein content and thick yolk structure, Samarth Eggs Center operates custom-built temperature-controlled hubs.

Our packaging units process up to 100,000 eggs per day, utilizing mechanical graders to separate sizes (Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large) and reject cracked shells instantly using light-candling inspections.
