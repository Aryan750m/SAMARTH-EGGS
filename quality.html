Quality Protocols

Uncompromising standards for safe, clean, and nutritious supplies.

Five-Step Verification Cycle

Every egg that leaves our distribution facility passes through these inspection checkpoints.

01

Automated Light Candling

Eggs run over high-intensity lighting tables to check internal yolk centering, identify micro-fractures in shells, and reject sub-standard eggs automatically. This guarantees shell integrity before packing.

02

Sanitized Washing

We perform washing operations utilizing eco-friendly, food-grade warm washes that strip external organic residues without compromising the natural protective cuticle barrier of the shell.

03

Weight & Sizing Calibration

Electronic grading scales weigh every egg to group them into standard market slots (Small: 45-50g, Medium: 50-55g, Large: 55-60g, Extra Large: 60g+). We guarantee consistent tray counts.

04

Bio-Security Farming

Our feed programs exclude chemical hormones or trace animal byproducts. Healthy grain feed ensures thick yolks and dense shell coatings naturally.

05

Cold-Chain Dispatch

All eggs are loaded into temperature-monitored vehicles to avoid thermal shock, locking in fresh nutrients until they arrive at your warehouse.
